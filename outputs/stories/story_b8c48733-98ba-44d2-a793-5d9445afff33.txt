Here is a short kids animal story based on the topic "The Magic of Sharing":

**Scene 1: Introduction to Benny the Bunny**

(Benny the Bunny is sitting in his cozy burrow, surrounded by toys and books. He looks happy but also a bit lonely.)

Narration: "Meet Benny the Bunny! Benny loves playing with his toys, but he has a big problem - he never shares with anyone."

(Image prompt: A picture of Benny sitting alone in his burrow, looking sad)

**Scene 2: Benny Meets Sammy the Squirrel**

(Benny meets Sammy the Squirrel at the park. Sammy is eager to play and share some acorns.)

Narration: "One day, Benny met Sammy the Squirrel. Sammy was so excited to show off his favorite acorns. Benny wanted to play with them too!"

(Image prompt: A picture of Benny and Sammy meeting in the park)

**Scene 3: Benny Learns to Share**

(Benny decides to share his toys with Sammy. They have a blast playing together.)

Narration: "But then Benny realized that sharing was more fun than keeping everything to himself! He shared his favorite toy car with Sammy, and they played together for hours."

(Image prompt: A picture of Benny sharing a toy car with Sammy)

**Scene 4: Benny Spreads the Magic of Sharing**

(Benny's friends start to notice how happy he is when he shares. They want to share too!)

Narration: "Benny learned that when you share with others, everyone gets happy! He started sharing his toys and books with all his friends at the park."

(Image prompt: A picture of Benny surrounded by his friends, playing together and sharing)

**The End**

This story aims to teach children the importance of sharing and kindness. By following Benny's journey, kids can learn that sharing is a powerful way to make others happy and create positive relationships.

Here is a short kids animal story that incorporates the topics of sharing, kindness, empathy, community service, and gratitude practice.

**Scene 1: Benny's Big Share**

Narration: "Meet Benny the Bunny. Benny loved playing with his favorite toy car in his cozy little burrow."

Image Prompt: A picture of Benny holding a small toy car, looking happy and content.

Text: "One day, Benny's friend Sammy Squirrel came to visit. He was excited to play with the toy car, but Benny said 'no' because he wanted to keep it all to himself."

**Scene 2: Benny Changes His Mind**

Narration: "But then Benny remembered what his mom had told him: 'Sharing is caring.' So he decided to share his toy car with Sammy."

Image Prompt: A picture of Benny smiling and offering the toy car to Sammy, who is excitedly taking it.

Text: "Benny was happy to see Sammy playing with the toy car. They took turns playing together and having so much fun."

**Scene 3: Benny Spreads Kindness**

Narration: "The next day, Benny wanted to do something kind for his community. He collected some fresh carrots from his garden and made a special treat for the animals at the animal shelter."

Image Prompt: A picture of Benny holding a basket of fresh carrots and smiling, with a picture of an animal shelter in the background.

Text: "Benny visited the animal shelter and left the carrots on the counter. The animals were so happy to have a tasty snack, and Benny felt proud of himself for doing something kind."

**Scene 4: Benny Practices Gratitude**

Narration: "That evening, Benny sat down with his family to have dinner together. He looked around the table and said 'thank you' for all the things he was grateful for."

Image Prompt: A picture of Benny sitting at a table with his family, holding hands and looking happy.

Text: "Benny's mom smiled and said 'we're glad you feel thankful for what we have.' And Benny knew that sharing, kindness, empathy, community service, and gratitude practice were all important parts of being a kind and caring friend."

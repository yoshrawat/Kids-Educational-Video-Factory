Here's a short kids animal story for the topic "Sharing is Caring" with four scenes, narration, and image prompts.

**Scene 1: Benny the Bunny Loves to Share**

Narration: "Meet Benny, a friendly bunny who loves to share his toys and treats with his friends."

Image Prompt: A colorful illustration of Benny the bunny holding a ball and smiling at his friends.

Text: "Benny had a big box of juicy carrots in his burrow. He wanted to play with his best friend, Rosie the rabbit, but he didn't want her to get upset if she couldn't have one."

**Scene 2: Sharing Makes Everyone Happy**

Narration: "So Benny decided to share his carrots with Rosie and her siblings. They all played together and had a wonderful time."

Image Prompt: A happy illustration of Benny sharing carrots with Rosie and her siblings, surrounded by butterflies and flowers.

Text: "When everyone shared their toys and treats, they felt so much happier than when they were playing alone. Sharing makes everyone feel special and loved."

**Scene 3: Friends Share Together**

Narration: "Benny's friends wanted to share their own toys and treats with him too! They had a big sharing party in the forest."

Image Prompt: A lively illustration of Benny and his friends sharing toys, playing games, and laughing together.

Text: "Together, they shared their favorite foods, played games, and took turns on the swings. Sharing is a fun way to make new friends and have adventures with them."

**Scene 4: Feeling Jealous? Let's Share Again!**

Narration: "Sometimes when Benny saw his friends playing together without him, he felt left out or jealous. But then he remembered what sharing was all about."

Image Prompt: A heartfelt illustration of Benny looking sad, but then smiling as he realizes the importance of sharing.

Text: "Benny knew that if he shared again, he would feel happy and included with his friends. So he took a deep breath, smiled, and joined in on the fun! Sharing makes everyone feel special and loved."

This story aims to teach children the importance of sharing and kindness, using a relatable animal character and engaging illustrations. The four scenes showcase different aspects of sharing, from sharing personal belongings to feeling included and happy when sharing with others.
